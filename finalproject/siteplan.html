Individual Project Site Plan

BonTech Digital Services

Site Name

BonTech Digital Services

The site name was selected because it clearly identifies the brand as a technology-focused digital services provider. The name is short, professional, and relevant to web development and technical support services.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide information about BonTech Digital Services and the digital solutions it offers. The site will describe available services, explain how BonTech helps individuals and small businesses solve digital problems, and provide a clear method for users to make contact.

Scenarios

The following scenarios represent questions that a typical site visitor from the target audience may ask:

  • What types of web development or digital services does BonTech provide?
  • How can I contact BonTech for help with my website or technical setup?

Color Scheme

The website will use a professional and minimal color scheme to support readability and branding. The selected colors will be applied consistently across the site.

Deep Blue (#1f3c88): Used for headings, navigation, and branding elements.

Light Gray (#f4f6fb): Used for page backgrounds and content sections.

Accent Orange (#f39c12): Used for buttons, highlights, and call-to-action elements.

Typography

The following fonts were selected to ensure clarity, accessibility, and a modern appearance:

  • Montserrat: Used for headings and section titles.
  • Roboto: Used for body text and general content.
